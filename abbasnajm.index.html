IMPACT OF TECHNOLOGY IN HUMAN LIVES

History of Technology

The history of technology is the history of the invention of tools and techniques and is one of the categories of world history. Technology can refer to methods ranging from as simple as stone tools to the complex genetic engineering and information technology that has emerged since the 1980s.

Technological Change

Technological change, defined by economists as a shift to a new production function resulting from an increase in the efficiency of a product or process, is the main source of economic growth. In strictly economic terms technological change is presented as a positive transformation that often arrives in waves of related innovations. Geographers, in contrast, are more cautious about technological change because they are also concerned with social and environmental impacts. The rise of patent activity and the associated search for technology rents has led to the differential impacts of intellectual property on cities and regions.
